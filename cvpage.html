Hello, I'm Aine O'Hagan. I'm an interaction design student from Belfast. I'm also a mother, freelance designer and a massive Beastie Boys fan.

I graduated in 2014 from Belfast Metropolitan College with a merit level HND in graphic design and decided to top it up to a degree.In 2014 I won the Lowry award for graduating at the top of my class.

I love illustration and my work usually consists of fashionable girls eating pizza.

I run a blog called Aine Oh that I use as a personal space online to post about things I'm interested in. Mostly its about fashion, beauty and crafts.This blog has taught me alot in terms of marketing and SEO. I also have a good understanding of social media and how to gain traffic.

Through my first year of interaction design I have learned so much about different design from all over the world. I love learning new things and want to continue to learn.
